❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid? ▾
Earnings are settled every week (Mon–Sun). Released funds can be withdrawn instantly via IMPS/UPI from the Payout screen.
What if a patient is unavailable? ▾
Wait at least 10 minutes, attempt 2 calls, then report via Incident Report. The order will be rescheduled automatically.
How is commission calculated? ▾
The platform retains 12% of the order value. Your net pay = order amount − 12% platform fee + any active incentives.
My order is stuck – what do I do? ▾
Use the Live Chat option above for real-time help. For emergencies, tap the SOS button on the active delivery screen.
How do I update my bank details? ▾
Go to Profile → Bank Account. Changes require KYC re-verification and take up to 24 hours to activate.
